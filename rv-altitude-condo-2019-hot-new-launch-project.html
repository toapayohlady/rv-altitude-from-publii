RV Altitude Condo 2019Q1 Hot New Launch Project

    January 5, 2019 By River Valley Romantic

    RV Altitude will be the latest new condo project to be launched in River Valley, Singapore. The most prominent selling/buying point of this condo is its location just opposite River Valley Primary School. Purchasing a unit at this development means parents can rest easy, letting their domestic helpers send and bring their precious kids to school and home. Plus, you can save on paying school-bus fees. Your helper can even cook and deliver nutritious meals to your darling child when he/she needs to stay later in school for extra-curricular activities. 

    RV Altitude was formerly known as RV Millenia Condo.

    Roxy-Pacific Holdings purchased the site in a private land sale. The showflat will be launched and ready for public viewing on 5 January 2019. It will be exciting to see the property market's response in the start of the new year.

    Already, the developers of 3 Orchard By-The-Park shocked/surprised the market by releasing a new phase of their units in December, a time when developers have traditionally held back launching their projects to avoid the end-year lull period - because many affluent families are travelling out of Singapore. 

    I'm sure the market is eager to see how the launch of RV Altitude will perform. If prices continue to climb, existing property owners will rejoice, investors will sigh and those who are still waiting to enter the market will need to be even more patient if they want to see prices fall, or bite the bullet and enter the market with their sums done sufficiently right.

    Let's wait and see what happens this weekend.
